Best Practices Guidelines

Caption Quality

Ensure your captions work for you by reviewing and editing for accuracy. Customise text size, colour, and background to meet different access needs. At Hear With Us, we value user feedback, partnering with communities to refine our captions for maximum access

Financial Support

Providing access to meetings or events is the event organiser's responsibility. As with any costs, communication access, like catering, should be built into the registration fees. In this way, all participants share the costs of access.
