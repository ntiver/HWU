ABOUT US

Hear With Us champions captioning.

Hear With Us practices the social model of disability to advance our area of impact. We accept that the human diversity includes people with disabilities, various degrees of hearing, languages and ways of learning.

We seek to challenge and change audio communications and environments to include a visual equivalent – through captioning – to enable more people to fully participate, experience and engage on an equal basis with others.

We believe there is a gap in accessing meaningful, accurate captions for time-sensitive material at the present time. This affects everyday lives and experiences.

Hear With Us is a registered not-for-profit organisation based in Brisbane, Australia. We work with our members, sponsors, patrons and other community organisations in our advocacy efforts.

Anyone is warmly welcomed to join us.
