OUR PATRONS

Dr. James Fielding

CEO and co-founder of Audeara

BIOGRAPHY

Dr James Fielding is the CEO and co-founder of Audeara. Dr Fielding studied business management and biomedical science at the University of Queensland (UQ). After working in finance and public relations in New York, he started his medical degree where he was awarded a scholarship to complete MBA Studies. James’ passion was an idea for a medical device that would make it easier for people to take hearing tests and in the process, they realised the benefit for people outside of the medical application and started developing Audeara: technology that uses your personal hearing profile to enhance your entertainment experience. Dr Fielding stepped away from full-time clinical medicine in February 2016 to dedicate himself to Audeara,which floated on the ASX in May 2021. He is an avid music fan and the drummer for band ‘The Dashounds’. He believes in increasing the awareness of hearing loss and implementing improvements to ensure that all people can feel connected to the people and experiences that bring them joy.
